Kevin Burke QC

Barrister & Solicitor

Admitted to the Bar in April 1971, Kevin practices extensively in criminal and civil litigation as well as providing individualized service for his many clients. He has argued cases in Alberta, New Brunswick, Newfoundland and Labrador, and before the Supreme Court of Canada. Kevin believes that each client is entitled to fairness and justice and consistently strives to achieve a result that is satisfactory to his clients.
