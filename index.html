Kevin A. Burke QC

Barrister & Solicitor

Kevin Burke is an attorney practicing extensively in criminal litigation. He was admitted to the Canadian Bar Association in 1972, and subsequently practiced as a Provincial Prosecutor in Nova Scotia. In 1983, he established Burke Thompson, where he was a Partner with Walter Thompson for 33 years. Kevin believes each client is entitled to fairness and justice, and applies these principles to every engagement. He has argued cases in Nova Scotia, Alberta, Quebec, New Brunswick, Newfoundland & Labrador, and before the Supreme Court of Canada.
